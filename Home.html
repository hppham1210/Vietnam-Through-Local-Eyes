Welcome to Vietnam

Vietnam is a country with more than four thousand years of history. Over time, it has been shaped by ancient dynasties, periods of foreign influence, and strong cultural traditions that still remain important today.

Vietnam has become one of the most well-known countries for travelers because of its beautiful landscapes, rich history, famous street food, and affordable travel experiences. From mountains and rice terraces to beaches and modern cities, Vietnam offers something memorable in every region.

This website introduces Vietnam through a local perspective. Use the navigation menu above to explore the north, central, and south regions, along with hidden gems and travel planning ideas.

History

Vietnam has a long and meaningful history that can be seen in its temples, architecture, traditions, and daily life.

Why Travelers Love It

Travelers love Vietnam for its food, scenery, cultural depth, welcoming people, and variety of experiences.
